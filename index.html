Field Notes

Who made this

Built by a runner,
for runners.

I ran my first half marathon because someone told me I probably couldn't. I've run three more since — including a 1:52 PR — because it turns out I can't stop.

I built these tools because I wanted them mid-race and couldn't find anything that wasn't bloated, covered in ads, or built for people who jog once a week. Run1% is what I wished existed.

Based in Eugene, Oregon. No accounts, no cookie banners, no ads. Just useful tools for people serious enough to go the distance.
