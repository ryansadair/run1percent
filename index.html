Who made this

Built by a runner,
for runners.

I'm a 4× half marathoner based in Eugene, Oregon — one of the greatest running cities in the world. I built these tools because I wanted them mid-race and couldn't find anything that wasn't bloated, ad-covered, or built for casual runners.

Run1% is free. No accounts, no upsells, no cookie banners. Just useful tools for people serious enough to go the distance.
